# 12 Butterflies — Brand System
**Version:** v1.0 — 2026-06-11
**Status:** Active | Substrate file
**Note:** Full visual assets pending Jason brand drop (logos, full palette, typography file). This file establishes the rules that govern those assets when they arrive.

---

## Name

**12 Butterflies** — always written as two words with numerals. Never "Twelve Butterflies", never "12butterflies" (one word) in visible copy.

---

## Palette direction

**Guiding aesthetic:** Warm European light. Lisbon afternoon. Not corporate. Not dark. Not clinical.

Target palette (to be finalised when brand assets drop):
- Warm whites and soft creams — base
- Earth tones — terracotta, sand, warm stone
- Accent: deep navy or soft forest green (not black, not corporate blue)
- No lavender, no periwinkle — those are One 2b colours
- No dark backgrounds for standard posts

---

## Typography direction

- Humanist sans-serif or warm serif — not geometric, not cold
- Heavier weight for short statements, lighter for body text
- No condensed or ultra-wide faces
- Final typeface selection pending brand drop

---

## Logo usage

- 12 Butterflies mark only — no combination with One 2b wordmark
- No insurer logos, no financial institution logos adjacent to any 12 Butterflies asset
- Jason's personal signature or handwriting element may appear if it exists in the brand kit

---

## Photography direction

- Jason in context: training, Lisbon, ocean, travel, practice
- Natural light, honest moments
- Not staged, not stock
- No sharp corporate aesthetic — this is personal life, not brand photoshoot

---

## What this identity never carries

- One 2b wordmark or colours
- IGI product language
- Investor-facing content
- GBP, USD projections, or financial figures
- Named insurance counterparties
