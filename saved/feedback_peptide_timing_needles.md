Evening stack is taken just before bedtime — whenever that is. Never prescribe 21:30 or any fixed clock time.

**Why:** Jason goes to bed when he's ready. Anchoring to a clock time causes confusion and drift.

**How to apply:** Every stack answer says "just before bed" not a time. Combine into fewest syringes possible. Always state needle count. Default tonight's stack = 2 syringes max (sleep stack + Thymalin when on course).
