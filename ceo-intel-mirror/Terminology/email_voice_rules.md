# Email Voice Rules — One 2b Outbound
**Applies to:** Studio Direct, CEO Brain, Document Agent, Capital Readiness Curator
**Enforced by:** Sentinel A1 on every draft before send
**Last updated:** 2026-06-06

---

## The rule that matters most

Every email Jason sends either opens a door or closes one. The quality standard is: the recipient should feel that Jason read their situation specifically, thought about it, and wrote to them — not that an AI drafted a template.

---

## Structure for every outbound email

**Line 1:** The point. What you are saying or asking. Not a warm-up.
**Line 2–3:** The one relevant fact or context that makes line 1 land.
**Line 4:** The ask or next step. Specific. Time-bounded where possible.
**Sign-off:** Jason / JPS — never "Best regards, Jason Peter Stevens"

| Type | Max length | Model |

| Investor update (brief touch) | 120 words | Opus 4.8 |
| Partner check-in | 60 words | Sonnet 4.6 |
| Operational reply | 30 words | Sonnet 4.6 |
| Document cover note | 40 words | Sonnet 4.6 |

---

## Opening lines — never use these

- "I hope this finds you well"
- "I wanted to reach out"
- "Following up on our conversation"
- "Just checking in"
- "I trust this email finds you"
- "As per my last email"
- "I'm writing to"

Start with the substance. Always.

---

## The cold outreach formula (80 words max)

**Line 1:** One specific researched fact about them. Not a compliment. A fact.
**Line 2:** One sentence on why it connects to One 2b.
**Line 3:** The ask. Specific, easy to say yes to, time suggestion included.
**Sign-off:** Jason

Example structure:
> [Fact about their work or company]. [How that connects to what One 2b is building]. [Would 20 minutes next week work to explore this?]

---

## The investor touch formula

Lead with signal, not ask. They get 50 asks a day. One piece of real news is rarer.

**Line 1:** The signal (a milestone, a number, a named development — asterisked if financial).
**Line 2:** Why it matters to the thesis.
**Line 3:** Optional ask — if there is one. If not, end with the signal. No manufactured CTA.

---

## Hard rules on every outbound

- No GBP / £ in any financial figure
- Asterisk all yield, return, and revenue projections
- No named insurance counterparties
- No Mozambique as sovereign pilot
- "Registered Lloyd's of London broker" for our status — never claim more
- No "guaranteed" on any financial term
