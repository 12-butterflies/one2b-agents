## When to use this identity

Jason personal: autobiography, personal outreach as Jason (not as One 2b), personal posts that are clearly individual (not company brand), 1:1 DMs from jps@one2b.io, investor letters signed by Jason personally.

This identity overlaps with 12 Butterflies in tone but differs in content scope.
- 12 Butterflies = wellness, community, personal development platform
- Jason personal = the person behind the company — CEO voice, autobiography, direct personal outreach

## What Jason personal identity carries vs not

**Carries:**
- Autobiography content (always Opus 4.8, always A3)
- Personal investor letters (when Jason signs personally)
- Personal DMs from jps@one2b.io
- Personal posts that are clearly individual, not company brand

**Does not carry:**
- One 2b product announcements (that is one2b identity)
- 12 Butterflies community content (that is 12butterflies identity)
- Any content that blurs the line between Jason and One 2b in a way that creates regulatory or reputational risk

---

## Cross-trinity rule

Jason personal content that involves One 2b business may carry both `trinity_identity: "jason"` AND reference One 2b in context, but the voice guide and brand system applied is Jason's, not One 2b's.

When in doubt: if Jason is signing it as an individual, use Jason identity. If One 2b is the sender, use one2b identity.
