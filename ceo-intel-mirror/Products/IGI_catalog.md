## What the instruments do

One 2b's data-linked insurance products wrap validated data assets with an insurance guarantee that:
1. Makes the Tesseract valuation defensible to banks, investors, and auditors
2. Enables the data asset to be lent against, invested in, and securitised
3. Provides the yield target* that makes data an investable instrument

One 2b is a registered Lloyd's of London broker. The instruments are underwritten through senior relationships with major global insurance conglomerates. We never name them externally.

---

## Phase 1 — Current catalog (blended instruments)

**Instrument type:** Blended data-backed insurance
**Coverage scope:** Data assets across multiple sectors (enterprise, sovereign, financial)
**Premium range:** 1–3%* annual on insured value
**Coverage period:** 12 months, renewable
**Currency:** USD for the insured value, EUR for premium billing
**Jurisdiction:** English law default; sovereign instruments require jurisdiction-specific review

**Eligibility criteria:**
- Asset must have a completed Tesseract valuation
- Data quality score above minimum threshold (defined in Tesseract methodology)
- Entity is not in active litigation relating to the data asset
- Jurisdiction is within approved coverage geography

**Ineligible (Phase 1):**
- Personal data without explicit data subject consent framework
- Data assets with active regulatory investigation
- Synthetic or AI-generated data presented as real

## Pricing principles

All pricing is indicative until confirmed by the broker layer (Sean Yeo's team). The IGI Agent generates indicative premiums only — the broker confirms.

**Indicative premium drivers (higher premium when):**
- Shorter data history (less provenance)
- Narrower data category (less liquid comparable market)
- Emerging market jurisdiction
- No current revenue stream from the data

**Indicative premium drivers (lower premium when):**
- 5+ years of clean, audited data history
- Clear comparable market transactions
- Established jurisdiction with data asset recognition (Isle of Man, UK)
- Active revenue from data licensing or commercialisation
