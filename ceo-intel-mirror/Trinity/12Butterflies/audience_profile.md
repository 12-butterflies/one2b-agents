# 12 Butterflies — Audience Profile
**Version:** v1.0 — 2026-06-11
**Identity:** 12 Butterflies personal brand
**Status:** Active | Substrate file

---

## Primary audience

People who are building something — a business, a life, a practice — and are interested in doing it with more depth and less performance.

They are not beginners. They have read the books and done the courses and still feel like something is missing.
They are drawn to founders who are honest about the cost of the work, not just the rewards.

---

## Demographics (broad)

- Age: 28–48
- Location: Global, but European and Asia-Pacific skew
- Life stage: Founder, builder, parent, someone in a significant transition
- Income: Comfortable — these are not people in survival mode
- Education: High — but more important, deeply curious beyond formal education

---

## Psychographics

**They value:**
- Authenticity over performance
- Depth over reach
- Practice over theory
- Relationships over networks
- Embodied knowledge (what the body knows) alongside intellectual knowledge

**They are tired of:**
- Hustle culture pretending to be self-care
- Influencers monetising vulnerability
- "Morning routines" as status signals
- Content that optimises for engagement instead of honesty

---

## What they follow Jason for

- He has done the work and is still doing it
- He does not pretend things are simpler than they are
- He is honest about the interior cost of building at this level
- He has a physical practice and he lives in Lisbon and it is a different kind of life

## What Studio Social produces for this audience

Posts that feel like a voice note from a thoughtful friend. Not advice. Not instructions. Observation, reflection, honest moment.

Length: Short to medium. Long only for genuine depth — not padding.
Format: Text-first. Images of Jason in context (training, ocean, Lisbon, travel) not stock imagery.
