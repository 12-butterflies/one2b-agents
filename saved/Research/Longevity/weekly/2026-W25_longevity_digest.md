## EMERGING SIGNALS

### SS-31 (Elamipretide) — First FDA Approval
FDA approved FORZINITY (elamipretide/SS-31) in October 2025 for Barth syndrome — the first-ever FDA approval of a mitochondria-targeted peptide. Supported by TAZPOWER trial muscle strength data. No aging indication yet. Strengthens the evidence base and compound legitimacy for Jason's SS-31 (2S10) use.

### Kisspeptin in Older Men
Karger Neuroendocrinology study: hypothalamic response to kisspeptin-54 is preserved in older men but post-injection testosterone rises are attenuated vs young men. Central axis is intact; peripheral conversion may decline. Supports using upper end of pulsatile dose range once Jason's KP10 IU is confirmed.

### GHK-Cu — 60%+ Decline by Age 60
innerbody.com 2026 guide quantifies the natural GHK-Cu decline: 60%+ by age 60. Lab studies confirm collagen increase up to 70%. Gene expression reset effect (aged cells reverting to younger expression patterns) documented. Strongly validates the twice-daily protocol.

### 5-Amino-1MQ + NAD+ Synergy
NNMT inhibition by 50AM directly increases intracellular NAD+ — additive with injectable NJ500. Timing discipline is critical: 50AM with food (insulin-mediated), NJ500 fasted. Jason's current protocol already separates these correctly.
