## Summary
Positioning Cascais, Portugal as a pilot for a space biosciences and longevity medicine innovation hub. The hub integrates space biosciences, longevity medicine, AI, blockchain, and smart cities. Municipal government interest is confirmed. A formal proposal is being developed by João Ruivo.

## Key elements of the proposal
- Space biosciences and longevity medicine as flagship research
- Data valuation hub — data as mortgageable asset
- EU/Portuguese government funding pathways
- NOVA University curriculum and senior scientist relocation
- Gulbenkian Institute for embryo shipment and storage
- Media partnership (documentary filmmakers, Netflix/HBO interest)
- Post-graduate education and micro-credentials
